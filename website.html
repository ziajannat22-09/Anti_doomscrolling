About This Journal

Doomscrolling is designed to keep you addicted. This little corner of the internet is designed to do the opposite — give you a meaningful, creative alternative every time you feel the urge to mindlessly scroll.

Each idea is designed to be completable in 15-60 minutes. No pressure, no perfectionism. Just create something with your hands, read something with your heart, or journal something from your soul.
